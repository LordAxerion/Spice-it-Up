[Gameplay] Hospital_MU 1.0

This mod adds 2 more hospitals / doctors.

the sanatorium:
---------------------
Reach of the emergency forces (in comparison to the original) : +500
Emergency forces (compared to the original) : +150% (5 units)
Emergency forces movement rate (compared to the original) : +35%
Emergency forces intervention speed (compared to the original) : +150
Reduces sickness (compared to the original) : +5%
Disease reduction range (compared to the original) : +500%.


the alternative practitioner:
---------------------
Reach of the emergency forces (compared to the original) : -16%.
Emergency forces (compared to the original) : +50% (3 units)
Emergency forces movement rate (in comparison to the original) : +15
Emergency forces intervention speed (compared to the original) : +50%.
Reduces disease (compared to the original) : -3%.
Range of disease reduction (compared to the original) : -15%.



The buildings were integrated into the original building menu of the hospital,availability in embesa.
